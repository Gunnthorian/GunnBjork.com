Miniature Thrust Vector Control (TVC) System

This project involved the designing, printing, and assembly of a sub-scale Thrust Vector Control (TVC) unit for model rocketry, designed with a compact 1.75-inch outer diameter. Unlike a fixed rocket motor, a vector thruster allows for active flight stabilization and maneuvering by physically tilting the motor's exhaust nozzle. By shifting the direction of the thrust relative to the rocket’s center of gravity, the system can correct for wind or execute programmed flight paths without relying on traditional aerodynamic fins.
